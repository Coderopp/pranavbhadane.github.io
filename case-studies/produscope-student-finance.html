The 'Hisaab' Problem

College students face unique financial challenges that existing apps don't address:

  • Irregular income and spending driven by peer pressure
  • Difficulty tracking expenses across UPI and credit
  • Lack of financial literacy making budgeting harder
  • No student-first financial products in the market
