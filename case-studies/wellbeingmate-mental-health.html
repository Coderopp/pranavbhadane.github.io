The Problem

Mental health describes our state of social, psychological, and emotional well-being‚Äîintegral to overall wellness. Yet workplace performance suffers significantly when employee mental health deteriorates.

What Causes Poor Mental Health at Work?

According to surveys, 57.9% of employees say work had at least a moderate impact on their mental health. Key factors include:

  • Inexperienced leadership and unclear communication
  • Long or inflexible work schedules with unrealistic demands
  • No sense of teamwork or community
  • Watching favoritism play out with coworkers
  • Lack of managerial support and resources

Business Impact

Poor employee mental health creates significant operational challenges:

  • 40% report revenue decreased
  • 39% see profitability decreased
  • 30% experience loss of customers
  • 26% report diminished output
  • 20% notice reduced competitiveness
"68% of 12,000 employees worldwide want to speak with a robot instead of a manager about their stressors at work."
